Moltke Crater

Stop and smell the exotic flowers...

Moltke Crater features some of the most unusual and esoteric botanical life known to this solar system. The crater's unique atmosphere and low gravity allow plants to grow and blossom unlike anything on Earth.

Find relaxing accomodations...

A single cabin - occupancy limit 1 - provides shelter from atmospheric stressors and a place to weather the cold nights. The finest, and only, lunar lodgings are located along the northwest ridge of the crater, over-looking Lake Luminos.
